Teams

Fourteen rosters. One crest.

Boys and girls, U8 through U19. Every team trains twice a week, competes on weekends, and travels together to showcase tournaments throughout the mid-Atlantic.

01 · Juniors

First touches.
Ages 5–8.

Small-sided play (3v3 and 4v4), once-a-week sessions, Saturday festivals. No travel tournaments yet — just lots of touches and plenty of goals.

Training · 1x / week Matches · Local festivals Season · Sep – Nov · Apr – Jun
