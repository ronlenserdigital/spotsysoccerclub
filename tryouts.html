How it works

Three evenings, one decision.

01

Register online

Takes about four minutes. We'll send a confirmation email with your evaluation dates, a medical release form, and directions to the fields.

02

Come play

Two 90-minute sessions per age group. Your player will train with current rostered players and be evaluated by two independent coaches. We look for technical ability, decision-making, and attitude.

03

Get your offer

Roster decisions are emailed within 72 hours of your second session. You'll have one week to accept and submit your first-payment.

Club fees

No surprises.

Club dues cover two practices per week year-round, league registration, referee fees, a complete kit (home, away, training top, shorts, socks), and field maintenance. Tournament fees are billed per event.

Common questions

What parents ask.

Do we have to attend both sessions?

We strongly prefer two sessions so coaches can see your player on different days with different groups. If you can only make one, email us beforehand and we'll work something out — but try for both.

What if my player is between age groups?

Birthdate determines age group per US Soccer rules, but we can play a strong player up a year at the coach's discretion. Come try out for the bracket that matches your birth year and we'll talk after.

Is there a tryout fee?

No. There is never a fee to try out at Spotsylvania SC.

My player has never done travel before. Are they welcome?

Absolutely. Many of our Academy (U9–U11) players are trying out for travel for the first time. We don't expect tryout-day performance — we're looking at trajectory and attitude.

When will we know if our player made the team?

You'll receive an offer (or a note about alternate options) within 72 hours of your second session. Offers are first-come, first-served within age groups, so please respond within a week.

What if we can't afford the club dues?

Tell us on the registration form. We have financial aid available for every age group — no player has ever been turned away for cost.
